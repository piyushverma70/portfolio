My Story

Hi, I'm Piyush Verma 👋

I'm a passionate web developer based in India, currently building my skills and reputation in the world of web development. My journey started with a curiosity about how websites work — and that curiosity turned into a commitment to mastering the craft.

I specialize in building clean, fast, and visually stunning static websites. My background in computers and a natural eye for design help me create digital experiences that not only work well — but look incredible too.

My first completed client project — a premium website for Kashish Masale — earned a perfect 5-star rating. That success fueled my drive to take on more projects, grow my skills, and eventually become a full-stack web developer.

I'm currently expanding into dynamic web development — learning JavaScript more deeply and exploring modern frameworks. Every day is a step toward mastery.
