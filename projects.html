The Challenge

The client needed a professional online presence for their traditional spice business — showcasing a catalog of 27+ products with a premium, trustworthy feel that would attract both local and online customers.

My Solution

I designed and built a multi-page static website with a "Royal Gold" theme — deep black backgrounds with golden accents to convey premium quality. The product catalog features interactive modals with pack size pricing, bulk inquiry forms, and was optimized for SEO and fast loading.
